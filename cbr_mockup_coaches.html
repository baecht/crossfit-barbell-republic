TRAIN WITH CERTIFIED
CROSSFIT COACHES

Every coach at Barbell Republic holds a CrossFit certification. Our team includes CF-L1, L2, and L3 certified coaches — because your training deserves qualified leadership.

WHAT DO THESE CERTIFICATIONS MEAN?

L1
CF-L1 — Level 1 Trainer

CrossFit Level 1 Trainer — The foundational coaching certification covering CrossFit methodology, movement standards, and class programming. This is where every coach starts.

L2
CF-L2 — Level 2 Trainer

CrossFit Level 2 Trainer — Advanced coaching skills including athlete assessment, movement correction, class management, and program design. Requires demonstrated coaching ability beyond foundational knowledge.

L3
CF-L3 — Level 3 Trainer

CrossFit Level 3 Trainer — The highest individual coaching credential. Requires demonstrated coaching excellence across all domains, peer evaluation, and years of verified experience.

Fewer than 2% of CrossFit coaches worldwide hold this certification.
